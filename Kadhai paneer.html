Kadhai Paneer Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 250 grams paneer (cottage cheese), cut into cubes
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
  • 1 capsicum, cut into cubes
  • 2-3 green chilies, finely chopped
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves)
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnishing

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in a kadhai (wok) or pan. Add cumin seeds and let them crackle.
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies. Sauté for a minute.
  4. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn soft and oil separates.
  5. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, and garam masala. Mix well.
  6. Add cubed paneer and capsicum. Mix gently to coat the masala.
  7. Crush kasuri methi between your palms and sprinkle over the kadhai paneer.
  8. Pour fresh cream and mix gently.
  9. Cook for 2-3 minutes on low flame. Adjust salt if needed.
  10. Garnish with freshly chopped coriander leaves.
  11. Serve hot with naan or rice.
