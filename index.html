Popular Recipes

Bhajiya

"Bhajiya," also spelled as "bhajji" or "bhaji," is a popular Indian snack or appetizer. It's essentially a type of fritter made by deep-frying sliced or battered vegetables, commonly served with chutney or sauce.
Dabeli

Dabeli is a popular street food snack originating from the state of Gujarat in western India. It is a flavorful and spicy dish that consists of a spicy potato filling stuffed between pav , along with various chutneys, peanuts, and crunchy sev.
Medu Vada

Medu vada, also spelled "Medhu Vada," is a popular South Indian snack or breakfast item known for its crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior. It's made primarily from urad dal batter, seasoned with spices and herbs, and deep-fried until golden brown.
Fafda

Fafda is a popular Gujarati snack known for its crispy texture and savory flavor. It's made primarily from gram flour (besan), seasoned with spices, and deep-fried until golden brown. Fafda is often enjoyed on its own or paired with other snacks like jalebi or chutneys.

Dosa

Dosa is a traditional South Indian crepe made from fermented rice and urad dal (black lentils) batter. It's a popular breakfast item and snack not only in South India but also across the Indian subcontinent and globally.
Jalebi

Jalebi is a popular sweet treat in Indian cuisine, particularly famous for its crispiness, syrupy texture, and distinctive spiral shape. It's often enjoyed as a dessert or snack and is commonly served during festivals and celebrations.

Kadhai paneer

Kadhai Paneer is a popular North Indian dish known for its rich and flavorful gravy, along with chunks of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and colorful bell peppers. It's a vegetarian dish that's often served in Indian restaurants and made at home for special occasions.

Khaman

Khaman, also known as Khaman Dhokla, is a popular savory snack from the Indian state of Gujarat. It's made from flour and seasoned with typically served as a breakfast dish or snack. Khaman has a texture and slightly tangy making it a favorite among both adults and children.
Idli

Idli is a popular South Indian steamed rice cake made from fermented rice and black lentil batter. It's a staple food in South Indian cuisine and is enjoyed as a snack, meal. Idli is known for its soft, fluffy making it a versatile dish that pairs well with a variety of accompaniments.
Paneer tikka

Paneer tikka is a popular Indian appetizer or snack made from marinated cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) and vegetables, which are skewered and grilled or baked until charred and flavorful. It's a vegetarian version of the traditional and it's known for its tangy flavors.
Khandvi

Khandvi, also known as Patuli or Suralichi Vadi in some regions of India, is a savory snack from Gujarat. It is made from gram flour and yogurt, and cooked to a soft, melt-in-your-mouth texture. Khandvi is characterized by its delicate, thin rolls that are often garnished with mustard seeds.
Paneer Bhurji

Paneer bhurji is a popular Indian dish made from crumbled paneer cooked with onions, tomatoes, and spices. It's a quick and easy dish to prepare, often enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or dinner. Paneer bhurji is known for its savory, spicy flavor and creamy texture.
